About Me

  • I am currently working as a research engineer at KMB Telematics Inc. We are making high-resolution imaging radar sensors for automotive cars! Yes! It's pretty cool.

  • In 2021, I finished my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Chicago under supervision of Prof. Mojtaba Soltanalian. I was a part of WaveOPT lab, UIC.

  • I received my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from West Bengal University of Technology, India in 2012.

  • Before joining UIC, I spent my time doing software designing, development, and maintenance as an IT specialist. I have almost one and half years of work experience in Cognizant Technology Solutions as a Java Developer during 2013-14.

  • Above everything, I am a part time artist and amateur photographer. My subject is mostly abstract, and black and white is my color.
